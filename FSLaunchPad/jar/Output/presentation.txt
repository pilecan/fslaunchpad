FSL is compatible with FS9 now.If your Flight Simulator is slow to start because you have a lot of sceneries, FsLaunchpad (FSL) is for you. FSL allows to Select your Sceneries, Load your Addons and Launch FSX in same time in few minutes. The version 1.20 allows to see your Source Addons alphabetically or by priority your and some new features. FSL User Interface is available in English and French. Visit www.fslauncher.com you will find videos on Youtube and more information about FsLaunchpad.
